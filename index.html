About the project

We are creating a game where the user has to destroy aliens spread throughout York at various landmarks, using fire engines.


The game will be created in Java, and designed for people to play at open days. This means it will be able to run on a standard desktop PC, and not take very long to play.

About the team

We are a group of 6 second-year computer science students at the university of York, working together on a project to create a game.


Throughout the year, this website will be updated to include our progress and the work we have done on the project.
